Tentative Agenda

This agenda is tentative and subject to change. More details, including additional speakers, coming soon.

The Clinnovation conference is a single-day single-track conference taking place on Monday, April 9, 2018 from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM at Suffolk Law School, Sargent Hall, 120 Tremont St., Boston, MA 02108. The conference will start with breakfast in the 1st Floor Function Room and end with a reception in the 5th floor Sky Lounge.

9:00 AM - 9:30 AM
Welcome & Breakfast

Continental breakfast and opening comments from Andrew Perlman Dean of Suffolk University Law School.

9:30 AM - 10:15 AM
LIT Lab (A Case Study in Clinnovation)

The Director of Suffolk's LIT Lab, David Colarusso, will introduce the Lab, followed by a student-led sharing of their work with real-world clients, including projects from document automation to data analytics.

10:30 AM - 12:00 AM
Law Labs Survey

Hear from leaders of innovative law labs across the country, including the Legal Design Lab at Stanford, the Access to Justice Lab at Harvard, the NuLawLab at Northeastern, The Law Lab at Chicago-Kent College of Law, and LabX at BYU Law. Panelists will present a brief overview of their programs which will be followed by a discussion exploring the role of innovation in legal education and practice, moderated by Suffolk's David Colarusso.

12:15 AM - 1:45 PM
Lunch & Keynote

The Honorable Ralph Gants, Chief Justice of the Massachusetts Supreme Judicial Court will speak.

2:00 PM - 3:30 PM
LIT Jobs

Listen to leading employers in the field of legal tech and innovation. Additional panelists will be named soon.

3:30 PM - 4:00 PM
LIT Bits

Rapid-fire talks (20 slides in five minutes). After the conference, a video of the presentations along with their authors' info will appear under LIT Bits. For an example of the format, check out the LIT Bits page. Now accepting proposals.

4:00 PM - 6:00 PM (5th Floor Sky Lounge)
Reception & Poster Session

Speak with practitioners and academics about their work while enjoying drinks and light hors d'oeuvres. Now accepting proposals. This is also a great chance to catch up with other attendees and speakers.
